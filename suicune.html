Suicune (Non-Shiny)

Suicune (Shiny)

Why this Pokemon?

Although not widely used in competitive play, I still quite enjoy this pokemon as my favorite legendary in the game. I like the relaxed look it has, and it's not super flashy like other pokemon designs so you can look at it for a while and still enjoy it without feeling like you need to look for some sort of secret on it's design. I still to this day do not know why I enjoy this Pokemon so much, but what I do know that visually in my opinion it looks way better than the other two legendary trio of the region.

Information/Comp Usage

Typing: Water

Ability: "Pressure" - Pokemone Lose more PP when using a move

Moveset: Substitute, Calm Mind, Scald, Icebeam

Prefered Held Item: Leftovers - each turn after both Pokemon attack, the pokemon regains a little bit of health

Role: Setup Sweep
