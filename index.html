About Me

Hello! And welcome to this new website. I created this site to share my passion for the popular video game series Pokemon, aswell as some other things in the future. But before we get to that i'll tell you how this game sparked my intrests. When I was about 6 or 7 I owned a Nintendo DS Lite, it had a slot at the bottom to input Gameboy cartridges. My older brother just so happened to have the cartridge for Pokemon Saphire. I begged him to let me play it and after a while he grew tired of playing the game, so I had taken it to play myself . That was my first time playing the game but I can still remember the joy of starting it up, with the Gameboy jingle playing as it started up. I sucked at it, but got better as time passed. and now I land here, where I have played many of the games, conducted shiny hunts, replayed, even taken on unique sets of rules called nuzlocke rules where if a pokemon faints you have to box it permanantly. Even playing fan-made games with interesting spins on the story and new pokemon people created. Which now brings me to the topic of this site.

About This Page

On this site i've decided on adding tabs about things that intrest me or got me into things that I currently like.The first thing I have decided to add is a list of my top six favorite pokemon (since six usually make up a party) as well as a competitive play guide which will be a video at the end once you're done with the rest of my site.
