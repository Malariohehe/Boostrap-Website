Kartana (Non-Shiny)

Kartana (Shiny)

Why this Pokemon?

I think kartan as a Pokemon is a very cool concept and is very widely used in competitive play. It's needless to say that this Pokemon is OverPowered in a sense I only just recently started using this pokemon and have already gotten many sweeps with it with its high speed and high physical attack.

Information/Comp Usage

Typing: Steel/Grass

Ability: "Beast Boost"- After KOing an enemy Pokemon attack goes up by X.5.

Move Set: The moveset for Kartana varies depending on who is using it. I prefer to use Sacred Sword, Leaf Blade, Smart Strike, and Arial Ace, While some people will sack a move for swords dance.

Prefered Held Item: Focus Sash - if pokemon is taken to 0 hp from max this item activates and keeps it alive at 1 hp.

Role: Speedy Physical Attacker/Setup Sweep
